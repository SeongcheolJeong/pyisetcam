Known Limits

  • Public seed profiles are not vendor sign-off databases. Accurate block latency requires BSP documentation, kernel trace, hardware counters, or bench measurement.
  • AF is intentionally out of scope in the current 3A layer.
  • The simulator models timing/control behavior; fixed-point vendor ISP numerical matching is a separate task.
